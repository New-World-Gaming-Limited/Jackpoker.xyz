Spin & Win

3-max hyper-turbo lottery SNGs running on demand around the clock. Pick your buy-in, spin the wheel, and play for a randomly assigned prize pool that could reach $1,000,000.

Spin & Win is as simple as tournaments get. Three players sit down, the prize pool multiplier is revealed, and everyone plays a hyper-turbo with a 500-chip starting stack at 25 big blinds. Games typically wrap up in about 15 minutes.

Buy-ins range from $1 to $100. At lower multipliers, winner takes all. At higher multipliers, second and third place also get paid, which gives more players a share of the bigger pools.

The top jackpot multiplier reaches $1,000,000, though higher multipliers are rare by design. The real appeal is the speed and variance: each game is a quick, self-contained session where a single spin can turn a $1 buy-in into a meaningful payout.

PKO (Progressive Knockout)

Progressive Knockout tournaments add a bounty layer to every MTT. Every player you eliminate pays you instant cash on the spot, regardless of where you finish overall.

In a PKO, half your buy-in goes into the prize pool and half becomes your bounty. When you knock someone out, you win half their bounty immediately as cash, and the other half gets added to your own head. The longer you survive and the more people you eliminate, the bigger the bounty on your head grows. PKO events at JackPoker run regularly on the daily schedule and feature heavily in special series.
