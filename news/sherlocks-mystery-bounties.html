Sherlock's Mystery Bounties: $2,500,000 GTD Mystery Bounty KO Series Launches at JackPoker

JackPoker has launched its most ambitious tournament series of 2026: Sherlock's Mystery Bounties, a 21-day Mystery Bounty Knockout extravaganza running from March 30 through April 19 with a staggering $2,500,000 in total guaranteed prize pools. The series features over 100 individual tournaments across every buy-in level, all using the increasingly popular Mystery Bounty format that adds a layer of suspense and potential massive upside to every single elimination.

What Are Mystery Bounties?

In a Mystery Bounty tournament, a portion of each player's buy-in goes into a bounty pool. When you eliminate an opponent after the bounty phase begins, instead of receiving a fixed bounty amount, you draw a random mystery envelope. That envelope could contain anything from the minimum bounty value all the way up to the maximum prize — which in this series reaches $4,500 or more. This means a single knockout in a $10 buy-in event could pay you more than the entire prize pool of a smaller tournament. The variance is part of the thrill, and it rewards aggressive play with the constant possibility of a massive payout.

Series Structure and Format

Sherlock's Mystery Bounties spans 21 days with 100+ events scheduled across multiple daily time slots to accommodate players in every timezone. Buy-ins range from micro-stakes entries as low as $2.20 all the way up to $215 high-roller events. Every tournament in the series uses the Mystery Bounty KO format — there are no freezeouts, no rebuys-only events. The consistency of format means once you understand how mystery bounties work, you can jump into any event in the series and know exactly what to expect.

Key events include multiple flagship tournaments with $100,000+ individual guarantees, daily grinders with $10,000–$25,000 guarantees at mid-range buy-ins, and an accessible micro-stakes track where even the smallest bankrolls can chase life-changing mystery bounty envelopes. Satellite events are also running throughout the series, allowing players to win their way into higher buy-in events at a fraction of the cost.

Instant Cash Payouts

One of the headline features of Sherlock's Mystery Bounties is the Instant Cash payout system. Every bounty you win is credited directly to your account balance in real time — there are no tickets to collect, no tournament dollars, no pending payouts. The moment you bust an opponent and reveal your mystery bounty, the cash is yours. This makes the series uniquely liquid: you can be playing a $22 event, knock out three players with generous mystery envelopes, and have that cash available to register for another event or withdraw immediately.

Player Promotions and Added Value

Beyond the $2.5 million in guaranteed prize pools, JackPoker is running several companion promotions alongside the series. Players on the JackPoker mobile app can also take advantage of mobile-exclusive satellite freerolls that feed directly into Sherlock's Mystery Bounties events, providing completely free paths into the series for app users.

How to Get Started

The series is already underway and runs through April 19, 2026. To participate, create your free JackPoker account, make a qualifying deposit, and navigate to the Sherlock's Mystery Bounties tab in the tournament lobby. Events fire throughout the day across all time zones, with peak scheduling during European and American evening hours. Late registration is available on most events, so even if you miss the start of a tournament, you can still jump in during the early levels.
