Push-Fold (All-In or Fold)

One decision per hand. All-in or fold. Maximum adrenaline with zero fluff.

No Limit Hold'em Unique Format 100 BB Stacks 6-Max

Push-Fold strips poker down to a single decision: jam the pot or throw your hand away. There's no preflop limping, no raises, no three-bets — you pick up your cards and either push all your chips in or fold. That's it.

It sounds simple, but there's genuine skill involved. Knowing which hands to shove and which to fold at different positions and stack depths is a real discipline, and the 100 BB starting stack with a 10 BB ante makes every hand consequential from the first seat.

Six players per table means you're acting frequently, and the pace of play is extremely fast. Sessions can be short and sharp. It's a great format if you've got 20 to 30 minutes and want real poker decisions without committing to a long cash game session.

Push-Fold at JackPoker is also an effective way to build volume quickly and work through bonus requirements without the slow grind of a full cash game.
